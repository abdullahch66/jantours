About us

Welcome to Jan Tours, your premier destination for car rental services across Pakistan. We pride ourselves on offering a diverse range of vehicles to meet all your travel needs. Whether you require a compact car for a day trip, a spacious van for group travel, or a luxury SUV, we've got you covered. Our fleet also includes well-maintained Coasters and Hi-aces, perfect for larger groups or corporate outings, and armored vehicles for those requiring extra security.

Mission

Jan Tours is dedicated to delivering safe, comfortable, and reliable transport solutions through a high-quality fleet and expert support.

Vision

To be Pakistan’s trusted name in passenger transport and logistics, serving local and international clients with safe, reliable, and high-quality solutions.
